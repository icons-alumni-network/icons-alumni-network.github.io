ABOUT US


Armed with four years of training in critical thinking, scientific problem solving, and communication, our members are ready to take on the world's biggest challenges.

This international network aims to foster and maintain a powerful sense of community among the alumni of iCons. We believe that we can carry the collaborative spirit of the program out into the world, and by doing so, improve both it and ourselves.

Our Values

Professional Development

iCAN members work in diverse industries, from biological research to clean energy to software development, and strive to be leaders in these fields. The primary function of iCAN is to foster the exchange of ideas, resources, and people between academic and industrial disciplines.

Social Networking

iCAN aims to bring our members together outside of work as well. We sponsor events, outings and gatherings that help us get to know each other.

Community Service

iCAN is proud to give back to the community. The network organizes various service events, usually with a technical or academic focus.
